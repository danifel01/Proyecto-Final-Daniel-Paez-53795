What do we give?

This section provides a broad overview of each product category, highlighting their respective roles and importance within our catalog. All the products are organized into 4 main categories which are the following:

  • 1. Medical products: These products encompass a wide range of provisions aimed at diagnosing, treating, and preventing illnesses and injuries.
  • 2. Memberships: Memberships typically involve subscription-based programs or plans that offer various benefits and services to members.
  • 3. Ancillaries: Ancillary services refer to supplementary healthcare services that support and complement primary medical and dental care.
  • 4. Dental: Dental services focus on the maintenance of oral health, including the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of dental issues.
