Medical Non-Deductible Products

Med-Fixed for Sickness:

* This is a medical plan offers a fixed amount of coverage tailored to meet your healthcare needs.

* It comes with exclusive discounted network rates on medical services, treatments, and procedures.

* Designed to provide coverage for minor illnesses.

* Offers quick and efficient reimbursement for claims covered under medical expenses.

Med-Fixed for Accidents:

* This part of medical plan works exactly as the section for sickness. The only difference is its coverage for injuries billed with a diagnosis code related to an accident.

* No deductibles and/or co-pays.

* Additionally, it offers excess medical coverage for any accident or injury approved under medical necessity.

Med-Fixed for Wellness:

* This part offers access to preventative care services annually, ensuring proactive health management.

* As well as the part for Sickness and Accidents, all these plans have no deductibles and/or co-pays.
