Medical Deductible Products

Med-Deductible for Sickness:

* It is subject to in and out-of-network deductibles, with co-insurances but no out-of-pockets.

* It also offers exclusive discounted rates on medical services, treatments, and procedures.

* It has coverage for spanning surgeries, durable medical equipment and skilled nursing facility care.

* Provides reliable coverage for inpatient services and emergency room visits.

Med-Deductible for Accidents:

* It offers coverage for injuries billed with a diagnosis code related to an accident.

* It also offers excess medical coverage for any accident or injury approved under medical necessity.

Med-Fixed for Wellness:

* This part offers access to preventative care services annually, ensuring proactive health management.

* It does not apply for sickness or accident deductibles and its accumulators.

Med-Fixed-Deductible:

* This plan is the upgraded version of Med-Fixed plans.

* It is subject to deductibles, with co-insurances but without out-of-pockets.

* It is tailored to your healthcare needs since it has coverage spanning surgeries, durable medical equipment, inpatient services, emergency room visits, and skilled nursing facility care.
